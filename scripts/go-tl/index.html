Hakbiit Timor-Leste

Bemvindu ba sentru rekursu komprensivu ida-ne'e ne'ebé dezeña atu apoia profesór sira, estudante sira no komunidade sira ne'ebé vibrante iha Timor-Leste. Koleksaun materiál ida-ne'e ho objetivu atu haburas dezenvolvimentu sustentável, interkámbiu kulturál, no aprendizajen mútua entre ema ne'ebé ko'alia Inglés no ema Timoroan sira.

Welcome to this comprehensive resource hub designed to support teachers, students and the vibrant communities of Timor-Leste. This collection of materials aims to foster sustainable development, cultural exchange, and mutual learning between English speakers and Timorese people.

All software on this site and content are associated with an open source github repo linked below. Story text and images in https://go-tl.com/stories/archive.html are AI generated and are free to use. Kódigu jeralmente disponivel liután bainhira haree fonte iha pájina.

Projetu ne’e dedika ba haburas dezenvolvimentu sustentável, prezerva kultura Timor nian, no harii relasaun ne’ebé dura entre ema hotu. Hodi tau hamutuk matenek tradisionál ho téknika modernu, mai ita kria futuru ne’ebé nabilan liu ba Timor-Leste.
